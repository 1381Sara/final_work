The Huda Beauty Journey

Huda Beauty was founded in 2013 by beauty influencer Huda Kattan, following her success with a beauty blog she launched in 2010. Starting with her passion for makeup, Huda set out to fill a gap she saw in the industry high-quality false eyelashes.Her first collection of lashes sold through Sephora Dubai and quickly gained worldwide attention. Over time, Huda Beauty expanded far beyond lashes, introducing a diverse line of cosmetics including liquid lipsticks, foundations, concealers, and eyeshadow palettes.
